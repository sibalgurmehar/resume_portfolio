BioSeek

Project Lead

A smarter way to search the world's biomedical literature.

The problem

Keyword search on PubMed misses papers that are conceptually related but worded differently — a real gap when the evidence you need doesn't share your vocabulary.

What I built

A full-stack search platform combining lexical, semantic, and hybrid retrieval, with cluster-based exploration so researchers can browse themes, not just ranked lists — surfacing more diverse and semantically coherent evidence than keyword search alone.

02

Emosense

Project Lead

Reading emotion from the body to support mental health.

The idea

Stress shows up in signals we don't always notice — heart rhythm, facial expression. What if a system could pick up on those and respond with something calming?

What I built

A stress-detection and mental-health support system fusing ECG (via Arduino), facial recognition, and a KNN classifier, paired with mood-based music recommendations and a chatbot. I owned end-to-end execution and coordinated the team.
